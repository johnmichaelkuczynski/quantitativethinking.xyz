YOU ARE GOING TO EMBED INTO THIS COURSE DIAGNOSTIC ASSESSMENTS. THESE ASSESSMENTS WILL BE IN THE AREA OF THE COURSE. SO IF THE COURSE IS AI 101, THEN YOU WILL BUILD IN VARIOUS DIAGNOSTIC ASSESSMENTS THAT TEST THE USER'S MASTER OF (OR, IF THE ASSESSMENT IS ONE OF THE EARLY ONES, APTITUDE FOR) AI. THERE WILL BE SEVEN SUCH ASSESSMENTS. ONE FOR BEFORE THE COURSE; ONE AFTER EACH OF THE FOUR WEEKS (EACH OF THESE WILL COVER THE PREVIOUS WEEK); ON AT THE END OF WEEK 2 THAT COVERS BOTH WEEKS 1 AND 2; AND ONE AT THE END OF WEEK 4 THAT COVERS WEEKS 1-4. (SO THERE WILL BE ONE SUCH DIAGNOSTIC AT THE BEGINNING OF THE COURSE, BEFORE WEEK 1; ONE AT THE END OF WEEK 1; TWO AT THE END OF WEEK 2, ONE OF THEM COVERING ONLY WEEK 2, THE OTHER COVERING BOTH WEEKS 1 AND 2; ONE AT THE END OF WEEK 3; AND TWO AT THE END OF WEEK 4, ONE OF THEM ONLY COVERING WEEK 4, THE OTHER COVERING WEEKS 1 THROUGH 4.) THE USER HAS TO TAKE THESE. BUT THEY ARE NOT GRADED. AS LONG AS HE TAKES AND COMPLETES THEM, THEN, NO MATER THE SCORE, HE GETS FULL CREDIT (WHICH COUNTS FOR 20% OF HIS GRADE.) BUT HHERE IS THE KICKER. NOT ONLY WILL THESE TESTS BE DIAGNOSTIC: THEY WILL ALSO BE INSTRUCTIVE; THE USER CAN TAKE THEM OVER AND OVER TO TEST HIS SKILLS. AND EACH TIME HE TAKES THEM, HE GETS AN ENTIRELY NEW TEST: NEW QUESTIONS. SAME KIND OF TEST, SAME FORMAT, SAME KINDS OF QUESTIONS; BUT NEW QUESTIONS. MOREOVER, THERE ARE FOUR VERSIONS OF EACH OF THESE DIAGNOSTIC TESTS. 1. MULTIPLE CHOICE. 2. WRITTEN ANSWER. 3. HYBRID. AND 4. OFFICIAL. THE FIRST THREE TESTS ARE SHORT; THE 'OFFICIAL' ONE IS TWICE AS LONG AS ANY GIVEN ONE OF THE OTHERS; AND THE OFFICIAL ONE IS ALSO HYBRID, I.E. BOTH MULTIPLE CHOICE AND WRITTEN. SO TECHNICALLY THERE ARE 28 DIAGNOSTIC TESTS (FOUR VERSIONS OF EACH OF THE AFOREMENTIONED SEVEN DIAGNOSTIC TESTS), WITH THE QUALIFICATION THAT THE USER IS ONLY REQUIRED TO TAKE THE OFFICIAL ONES. THE APP TRACKS THE USER'S PERFORMANCE ON THE DIAGNOSTICS; AND THERE IS A FUNCTION "BUILD AN ASSESSMENT THAT IS SPECIFIC TO MY NEEDS' FUNCTION THAT LETS THE USER BUILD AN ASSESSMENT THAT TESTS WHAT HE MOST NEEDS TESTING ON AND DOESN'T WASTE HIS TIME ON MATERIAL HE HAS MASTERED. THE APP TRACKS THE USER'S PERFORMANCE ON THESE CUSTOMIZED TESTS. (THE USER'S PERFORMANCE ON THESE CUSTOMIZED TESTS WILL BE TRAKCED BY THE APP AND INCORPORATED INTO HIS PROFILE.) THE USER CAN SET THE SCOPE OF THE CUSTOMIZED TESTS; HE CAN SAY, FOR EXAMPLE, "IT SHOULD COVER ONLY WEEK 2" OR "IT SHOULD COVER ONLY ISSUES FROM WEEK 3 AS THEY RELATE TO LINEAR ALGEBRA" OR WHATNOT.
